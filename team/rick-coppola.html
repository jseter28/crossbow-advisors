Richard Coppola

Senior Advisor

Richard Coppola is a Senior Advisor — Capital Markets and Portfolio Management with Crossbow. Rick leads the firm’s underwriting and asset management efforts for Crossbow’s clients and partners. In this capacity, he interfaces directly with developers, investors and operating partners on a variety of issues.

From 2016–2019, Rick was a senior consultant with Clarion Partners, a $50 billion global institutional real estate investment manager. In this role, Mr. Coppola worked with Clarion’s senior management to strengthen and expand all aspects of their real estate lending platform.

Prior to working with Clarion, Mr. Coppola was Managing Director and Head of Capital Markets for TIAA-CREF (now Nuveen). Rick spent thirty years with TIAA-CREF, holding senior management positions across the firm’s institutional real estate group. Most recently, Rick was responsible for all debt and equity activity undertaken by the platform, including the financing of acquired/owned assets and the extension of credit/mortgages (senior, mezzanine and preferred equity) to third-party borrowers, all of which averaged $15 billion per annum in recent years.

During his tenure with TIAA-CREF, Mr. Coppola held a series of senior investment and executive management positions with significant authority for approving all new investments. Rick was a member of the firm’s executive leadership team that oversaw all real estate debt and equity business in the U.S., built and managed a high performing national team in multiple locations across the nation and successfully integrated the views and investment acumen across the four disciplines of lending, borrowing, acquiring and selling of assets.
