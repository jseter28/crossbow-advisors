Sanjeev Handa

Senior Advisor

Education
Undergraduate degrees from The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art (Civil Engineering) and New York University (Applied Science); MBA, University of Michigan.

Sanjeev Handa is a Senior Advisor at Crossbow. In this capacity, Sanjeev contributes to the overall strategic focus and development of the firm, manages key client relationships, and assists in the execution of important mandates.

In addition to his work at Crossbow, Sanjeev is an Independent Board Director of Fitch Ratings, Inc., and its European counterpart, Fitch Ratings, Ltd., where he serves as Chairman of their combined Compliance Committee. Sanjeev is also on the Board of Directors of GreenPath Financial Wellness and serves on its Executive Committee. Additionally, Sanjeev is a member of the Investment Committee of the Board of Trustees of The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art.

From 1988–2014, Sanjeev held a series of investment and executive management positions at TIAA-CREF. From 2011–2014, he was the Head of Commercial Real Estate Fixed Income and led the global expansion of the company’s private commercial real estate debt business. From 2006–2011, Sanjeev was the Head of Public Fixed Income with responsibility for the insurance company portfolio and the fixed income variable annuity and mutual fund businesses.
