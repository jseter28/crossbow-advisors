Who We Are

Crossbow Capital Advisors is a real estate capital advisory firm working at the intersection of institutional capital and the platforms that deploy it.

We advise institutional investors, family offices, and sponsors on capital formation, structuring, and the long-term alignment of interests across the capital stack. Our work is defined by precision, discretion, and execution quality — not volume.

Engagements are selective and relationship-led. We operate as an extension of our clients' senior teams, bringing institutional judgment to the decisions that shape a platform's trajectory.
